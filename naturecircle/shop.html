How to Use

1
Cleanse Your Skin

Start with clean, dry skin. Use your favorite gentle cleanser and pat dry with a soft towel.

2
Apply 2-3 Drops

Warm 2-3 drops between your palms. A little goes a long way with this concentrated formula.

3
Gently Massage

Press gently into face and neck using upward motions. Focus on areas of concern like fine lines.

4
Allow to Absorb

Let the oil absorb for 5-10 minutes before applying other products or makeup.

Pro Tips for Best Results:

  • Morning Routine: Use 2-3 drops before SPF for added protection
  • Evening Routine: Apply generously as the last step for overnight repair
  • Sensitive Skin: Start with 1 drop every other day, gradually increase
  • Mixing: Blend with your moisturizer for lighter application
  • Storage: Keep in cool, dark place for maximum potency

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this product safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, our Sacred Frankincense Face Oil is formulated specifically to be gentle on all skin types, including sensitive skin. Frankincense has natural anti-inflammatory properties that can actually soothe irritated skin. However, we always recommend doing a patch test before first use.

How long will one bottle last?

With twice-daily use (2-3 drops morning and evening), one 30ml bottle typically lasts 2-3 months. A little goes a long way with this concentrated formula, so you may find it lasts even longer with moderate use.

Can I use this oil under makeup?

Absolutely! Apply 2-3 drops in the morning, allow 5-10 minutes for absorption, then proceed with your usual SPF and makeup routine. The oil absorbs beautifully and creates a smooth, hydrated base for makeup application.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product (even if opened and used) for a full refund within 30 days of delivery.

How should I store this product?

Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight. Room temperature is perfect. The dark glass bottle helps protect the oil from light degradation. Proper storage will maintain the oil's potency for up to 2 years.

Is this product cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes, we are 100% cruelty-free and never test on animals. This product is also vegan-friendly - it contains only plant-based ingredients: frankincense essential oil, jojoba oil, and natural vitamin E.
